What most merchants miss about fuel surcharges

Fuel is one of the largest variable costs on your carrier invoice. Some carriers discount fuel for contracted shippers. Others do not. Some bake it into your rate tier so you never see it as a separate line.

Part n Parcel knows which is which, and uses that to get you the best possible overall cost.

How does this tracker work?

Fuel surcharges reset based on diesel and jet fuel prices published by Natural Resources Canada, the U.S. Energy Information Administration, and Kalibrate Technologies. Each carrier uses its own methodology and reset cadence.

Every row carries its own verified date so partial failures are visible, not hidden. A green dot means the source was successfully read this week. Yellow means the row is using the last known value because the source could not be reached on the latest run.

Click any carrier name to verify the rate against their published page. Part n Parcel is not affiliated with any carrier.
